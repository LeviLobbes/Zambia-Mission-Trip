Our Mission

Join the Baylor Business department on our mission trip to Zambia, partnering with Family Legacy. Young Zambians completing their education often face a job market with nearly 45% unemployment and little guidance. As many are the sole supporters of their families, our work helps them prepare for careers, creating brighter futures for themselves and their communities. Baylor students facilitate workshops on sales, customer service, interviewing, business plan writing, business ethics, and presentation skills. On the final day, we host a career fair, guiding Zambian students through interviews, aiming for immediate employment opportunities.

Our Partner: Family Legacy

Family Legacy Missions International is a ministry dedicated to transforming the lives of vulnerable children in Zambia through holistic care. Their programs focus on spiritual growth, education, physical well-being, and emotional support, empowering over 13,000 children annually through initiatives like Camp Life and Excel Beyond. Baylor partners with Family Legacy to deliver career workshops and support Zambian youth in building sustainable futures. Learn more at Family Legacy.
