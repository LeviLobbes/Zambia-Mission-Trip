By Ella Hanley | August 1, 2025 | Hamad International Airport, Doha, Qatar

After a long overnight flight, the Zambia team has officially landed in Doha! While we wait for our connecting flight, we have about five hours to explore the vibrant atmosphere of Hamad International Airport. We’ve spent the time looking through high-end shops, sampling local and international food, and getting a small taste of Qatari culture.

From art installations to unique flavors, it’s been an enjoyable layover—and a perfect way to stretch our legs before the next leg of our journey.
